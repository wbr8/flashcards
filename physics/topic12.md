Q: What two things does the future of the Universe depend on?
A: 
- The mass of the Universe
- The density of matter

Q: What effects might gravity have on the future of the Universe?
A: It might be the force that slows the expansion of the Universe, possibly causing it to contract inwards.

Q: What is the Big Crunch?
A: The expansion of the Universe slows down to a stop, then starts to contract inwards.

Q: What is the flat Universe?
A: The expansion of the Universe will stop, and the Universe will remain the same size.

Q: What is the closed Universe?
A: The expansion of the Universe will slow down to a stop, then start collapsing.

Q: What is the open Universe?
A: The expansion of the Universe will continue forever.

Q: After measuring the rotational speed of stars in galaxies, what did scientists find that they were not expecting?
A: The stars in the galaxies tend to rotate at similar speeds, whatever their distance from the galactic centre.

Q: What is Dark Matter?
A: Mass that does not emit light, but must exist to create the centripetal force that keeps galaxies spinning.
